My Education

KINDERGARTEN

Name of kindergarten Year About
Tadika Maria 2007-2008 Tadika Maria is a kindergarten that is located in Kajang, Selangor. When I was a student there, the kindergarten used to be next to a commuter rail so we often hear the sounds of commuters passing by. Now, it is located somewhere else because the previous spot is now part of the MRT station. The kindergarten is also attended by my brother and my second oldest sister when they were younger as well.

PRIMARY SCHOOL

Name of school Year About
Sekolah Kebangsaan Jalan Empat (JEPS) 2009-2014 JEPS is a primary school that is located in Seksyen 4, Bandar Baru Bangi, which is near my house. It is also often used as a voting centre for Bangi whenever there is an election. Out of all of my siblings, only my brother attended JEPS while my older sisters were students of Sekolah Kebangsaan Jalan Tiga (SEJATI).

SECONDARY SCHOOL

Name of school Year About
Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jalan Tiga (JATISS) 2015-2019 JATISS is a secondary school that is located in Seksyen 3, Bandar Baru Bangi, which is next to Sekolah Kebangsaan Jalan Tiga (SEJATI). Most SEJATI students would attend JATISS during their teenage years. Unlike kindergarten and primary school, all of my siblings, and myself included, attended JATISS as our secondary school. I had many fond memories during my time as a student there.

UNIVERSITY

Name of university Year About
Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan (UiTMCK) 2020-now I am currently pursuing studies in Diploma in Information Management and now, I am in my final semester. I enjoy being here because Kelantan is known as a foodie's paradise. I can get a variety of foods at a low cost.
